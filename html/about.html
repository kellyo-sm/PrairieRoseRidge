    • Welcome to Prairie Rose Ridge

    We are a small family homestead located in the beautiful Southern Black Hills. We offer dry camping with easy access, wide campsites and room to relax. Enjoy viewing the local wildlife, birdwatching (the mountain bluebird is my favorite), or just soak in the South Dakota sunrise before your adventure!




    We are a convenient distance from many local attractions.




    Mount Rushmore: 43 miles/(52min)

    Crazy Horse: 30 miles/(41min)

    Custer State Park: 28 miles/(32min)

    Wind Cave: 19 miles/(22min)

    Mammoth Site: 15 miles/(15min)

    Mickelson Trail (Minnekahata trailhead): 4 miles/(5min).
